Types of Elements

1. Block level elements: Occupies the space of the parent element.

    Eg. Div, Para, Header, 

2. Inline Elements: Occupies the space of the content of a element.

    Eg. Image, Anchor

Float: Displays the content side by side.

    > Converts block level elements into inline elements.

    Syntax:

        float: left | right;

        left - Starts displaying the elements from left side.

        right - Starts displaying the elements from right side.


        Margin-left + padding-left + border-left + width + Margin-right + padding-right + border-right
