Text Alignment: Aligns the text in different positions.

    Syntax:

        text-align: left | center | right;

Padding: Applies empty space between content and border.

    Syntax:

        padding: 10px;

            padding-left: 10px;
            padding-right: 10px;
            padding-top: 10px;
            padding-bottom: 10px;

Margin: Applies empty space between border and other tags.

    Syntax:

        margin: 10px;

            margin-left: 10px;
            margin-right: 10px;
            margin-top: 10px;
            margin-bottom: 10px;

Border: Applies border for the tags.

    Syntax:

        border: border-width border-style border-color;

        border-left: border-width border-style border-color;

        border-right: border-width border-style border-color;

        border-top: border-width border-style border-color;

        border-bottom: border-width border-style border-color;

Box Model

    Margin -> Border -> Padding -> Width;

    Margin -> Border -> Padding -> height;

Color:

        color: white
